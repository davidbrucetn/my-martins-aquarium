Tips

Your aquarium will need regular cleaning eventually, but not right away. It’s best to allow it to settle in and find its balance during the first few weeks.
Remember that an aquarium is a living system, and it takes time to establish the balance that will keep its inhabitants thriving.
Put your aquarium light on a regular on/off cycle to give your fish some down-time and prevent unsightly algae growth.
